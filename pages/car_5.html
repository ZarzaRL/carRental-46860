Mclaren | 540C | 2018

Info

Hire a McLaren 540C and you won´t be left wanting. You´ll be rewarded with a perfectly balanced sports car that combines blistering speed, effective handling and impressive steering that really puts you in tune with the road.

This mid-engined, 3.8 litre, V8 powered machine also enjoys scissor doors, a swooping nose, and a carbon fibre “Monocell” chassis that make it a stand-out car in its class. And, with a 0-60mph speed of 3.5 seconds it certainly enters supercar territory, despite McLaren classing it as part of their sports series. Moving through to the interior, it is more spacious and comfortable than you might first expect because even with its epic performance spec, McLaren also intended the 540C to be a daily driver. The seats are supportive, there is ample head clearance, but despite this practicality it still retains a sleek, sport and stylish feel. The traditional dials have been replaced with a configurable TFT display and it also benefits from McLarens own portrait orientated infotainment system, called IRIS.

This stylish and impressive sports car is more than a weekend-only adrenaline rush, it is an advanced performance machine that allows you to enjoy a long drive in comfort, whilst still getting your pulse racing.
